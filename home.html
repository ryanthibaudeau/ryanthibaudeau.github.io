// The Scientist

I'm a propulsion engineer and PhD researcher at Utah State University's Propulsion Research Laboratory, where my work centers on advancing chemical propulsion systems — particularly hybrid rocket engines and solid fuel ramjets.

Alongside my academic work, I contribute to propulsion development and additive manufacturing innovation at Space Dynamics Laboratory, applying research insights to real engineering problems and mission-critical systems.

My passion lies in understanding combustion dynamics, fuel regression, and the fluid-thermal interactions that define propulsion performance at the boundary of what's possible — across both atmospheric and in-space domains.
